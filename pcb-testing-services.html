PCB Testing Capabilities

Hands-on bench work across the full range of board-level testing and validation. Clear answers, documented results, and a path forward.

Bring-Up
PCB Bring-Up & Functional Validation
First power-on testing. Rail validation, current draw, peripheral init, reset sequencing, bus verification.
Investigation
Failure Analysis & Root Cause Reporting
Structured diagnostic process. Visual inspection, power-up characterization, signal capture, thermal imaging.
Instrumentation
Signal Integrity Testing
Oscilloscope capture, rise/fall time, overshoot, crosstalk. Clock signals, data lines, power rails.
Instrumentation
Power Integrity Testing
Rail noise, load transient response, sequencing verification. Thermal imaging for hot spots and current crowding.
Debug
Embedded Debug
J-Link debugger for SWD and JTAG targets. Step-through debug, register inspection, firmware validation.
Diagnosis
RMA Diagnosis
Field returns worked through structured diagnostics. Root cause documented with photos and recommended next steps.
Validation
Continuity & Shorts Testing
Board-level continuity, short circuit identification, net resistance measurement. Bare board and post-rework validation.
Rework
Post-Rework Validation
Boards validated after rework before returning to service. Confirms rework is correct and board passes functional test.
Support
NPI Support
Prototype bring-up through early production validation. Hands-on support when boards are newest and most likely to surprise.

Common Questions

Do you work with companies outside your area?

Yes. J-Tech LabWorks operates on a ship-in model and works with engineering teams nationwide. You ship hardware and documentation, we do the work, and results come back fast.

How does the ship-in model work?

You ship the hardware along with relevant documentation — schematics, complaint description, or test procedure for validation work. We perform the hands-on work and return results with clear documentation and photos.

Do you take small or rush jobs?

Yes — most small projects are completed in 1–3 business days. We'll confirm scope and timeline up front.

Will you sign an NDA?

Absolutely. We work NDA-friendly by default and can use your standard NDA or provide ours.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

A brief description of the board, the issue or test requirement, and any available schematics or drawings. The more context the better, but a quick description is enough to get started.

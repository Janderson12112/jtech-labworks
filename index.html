Minnesota FAQs

Do you take small or rush jobs?

Yes — most small projects are completed in 1–3 business days. We’ll confirm scope and timeline up front and include clear pass/fail results with inspection photos.

Can we ship parts in, or is this local only?

Either works. We handle ship-in or local drop-off. For ship-ins, we’ll share packing guidance and include photos in the final report.

Will you sign an NDA?

Absolutely. We work NDA-friendly by default and can use your standard NDA or provide ours.

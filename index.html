Common Questions

Do you work with companies outside your area?

Yes. J-Tech LabWorks operates on a ship-in model and works with engineering teams nationwide. You ship boards and documentation, we do the work, and results come back fast.

How does the ship-in model work?

You ship boards along with relevant documentation — schematics, complaint description, or test procedure for validation work. We perform the hands-on work and return results with clear documentation and photos.

Do you take small or rush jobs?

Yes — most small projects are completed in 1–3 business days. We'll confirm scope and timeline up front.

Will you sign an NDA?

Absolutely. We work NDA-friendly by default and can use your standard NDA or provide ours.

About J-Tech LabWorks

J-Tech LabWorks is a contract electronics lab serving hardware engineering teams nationwide, specializing in PCB bring-up, failure analysis, embedded debug, signal and power integrity, and NPI support.

Founded and run by Jerry Anderson, a hands-on electronics professional with experience supporting R&D, product development, validation, and sustaining work at Fortune 500 consumer electronics companies and medical device manufacturers.

The lab is equipped for serious bench work: Keysight DSOX1204G oscilloscope, dual logic analyzers, Keysight 34972A data acquisition system, thermal camera, spectrum analyzer with near-field probes, dual bench power supplies, programmable DC load, J-Link debugger, precision soldering and hot air station.

The focus is practical, execution-first support with clear communication and fast turnaround. NDA-friendly by default. Ship-in model, works nationwide.
